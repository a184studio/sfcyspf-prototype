    Introduction:

    Changes:

    • Added: (3 March 2020) Content for later half of jounrey
    • Added: Bereavement agent actions. The use of Bereavement grey box is against my recommendation.
      No clear call to action indication visually or written. In this context what does the (!) mean, what alternative behaviour for the agents are we trying to encourages?

      The use of this is visually jarring when it comes to iterations, e.g. radio sets and especially vast address radio sets.
      Reduction in contrast ratio of radios and hint text. Not sure if it’s appropriate to be adding a background colour to radio sets.
      Light-grey used isn’t always visible on DOI machines in TyneView Park.
